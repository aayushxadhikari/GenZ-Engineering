Renovation FAQs

Common questions we receive from renovation clients.

If your building is old, has major cracks, structural distress, or was built without engineered design, a structural assessment can determine if retrofitting is recommended.
Many renovations can be phased to minimize disruption. For major structural changes, temporary relocation may be safer.
Warranty depends on the system used and site condition. We explain options clearly in the proposal (materials + method + duration).
Timelines vary by scope. Small interior upgrades can take weeks, while major retrofit and structural work may take longer.
